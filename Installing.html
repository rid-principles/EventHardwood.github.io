Refinishing Process

Refinishing is a process where floors that have been either sanded or treated, or just sanded, are coated with stain. This is usually the final step of the process because once a floor is stained, the floor is complete and ready to walk on once it dries. Event puts unparalleled effort into all of the steps of creating a floor the customer has requested.
