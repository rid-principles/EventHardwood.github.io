Sanding Floors and Stairs

Home Page

Sanding is the first step to refinishing stairs and floors in the future. Sanding can be done more than once to ensure that all of the previous stain has been removed. Once this is done, if you want to change the color of the stain, a wood conditioner must be applied before the new stain is applied. Lastly, the finish is applied and the process can be repeated multiple times.
